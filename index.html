Travel to Japan

1st travel destination:
Kyoto

City in Japan

Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city on the island of Honshu.
It's famous for its numerous classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses.

3rd travel destination:
Osaka

City in Japan

Osaka is a large port city and commercial center on the Japanese island of Honshu.
It's known for its modern architecture, nightlife and hearty street food.
The 16th-century shogunate Osaka Castle, which has undergone several restorations, is its main historical landmark.
